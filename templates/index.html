Groundwater Commons Game

An interactive simulation exploring the tragedy of the commons through groundwater extraction. Choose your pumping strategy and observe how individual decisions affect shared resources over time.

Solo Mode

Control all wells yourself and explore the dynamics of groundwater extraction at your own pace.

Group Mode

Join a multiplayer game with your classmates. Compete for profits while sharing the same aquifer.
