Instructor Guide

Group Mode Setup

  1. Navigate to /group and click the "Host Controls" tab
  2. Adjust parameters if desired (defaults work well for most classes)
  3. Click "Create Room" to generate a 5-character room code
  4. Share the code with students
  5. Once students have joined, click "Start Game"
Students join at: Navigate to /group → "Join as Player" tab → Enter room code and name

Game Flow

Each round:

  1. Students choose their pumping rate (0 to qmax)
  2. When all students submit, the round automatically advances
  3. Results update in real-time for everyone

Host controls:

  • Force Advance: Move to next round without waiting for all submissions
  • End Game Now: Finish immediately and display final results

Solo Mode

Students can practice independently at /solo. They control all wells and can experiment with different extraction strategies. Useful for pre-class exploration or post-class analysis.

Key Parameters

Essential parameters to understand:

  • S₀ (Initial Stock): Starting aquifer level
  • R (Recharge): Natural replenishment per round
  • T (Rounds): Game length (default: 8)
  • qmax (Max Pumping): Maximum extraction per player per round

Discussion Questions

  • Did the aquifer deplete? Why or why not?
  • Would cooperation have increased total profits?
  • How did your strategy change as the stock decreased?
  • What policies could prevent overextraction?
